Milene Lopes portfolio website

Open index.html in your browser.

To make Instagram/LinkedIn videos playable inside the website:
1. Download your own videos from your Instagram/LinkedIn accounts.
2. Put them in the media folder.
3. Rename them to add-your-video-1.mp4 and add-your-video-2.mp4, or update the video src names inside index.html.

I could not automatically pull private Instagram/LinkedIn photos or videos into the website from here, so I left clean upload slots where you can add them.
